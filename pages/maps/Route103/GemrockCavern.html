Gemrock Cavern

A cramped cave with crystals jutting from the walls. Large boulders have fallen from the walls and ceilings, and it appears they can be broken with ROCK SMASH. There may be special Pokemon hiding underneath, or you may be able to find some treasures in the rubble.

Location: Route 103

Special Effects: Rocks have a 20% chance to drop an evolution stone.
